## What's Included

### Core Systems ✅
- Character creation with 7 classes
- Turn-based THAC0 combat
- Spell system (8/10 spells functional)
- Item and equipment management
- Light/darkness mechanics
- Trap detection
- Rest and healing
- Save/load system

### Content ✅
- 3 complete adventures
- 10 unique monsters
- 10 level 1 spells
- Starting equipment sets
- Sound effects
- Reference tools (dice roller, bestiary)

### Adventures ✅
1. Your First Adventure (Tutorial) - 5 rooms
2. The Goblin Warren - 5 rooms
3. The Haunted Crypt - 4 rooms

**Total Playtime**: 2-4 hours
